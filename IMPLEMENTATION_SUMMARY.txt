================================================================================
KNOWN LIMITATIONS & NOTES
================================================================================

1. Linux: May require manual VLC installation
   $ sudo apt-get install vlc

2. macOS: First run may require security approval
   System Preferences > Security & Privacy

3. Video Formats: LibVLC supports most formats, but codec availability
   varies by platform and installed codecs

4. Deletion: Permanent - no recycle bin/trash support
   (By design for adult content privacy)

5. Large Files: Hashing large files (>4GB) may take time
   Progress bar shows status
