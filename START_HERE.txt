================================================================================
FIRST-TIME USAGE
================================================================================

1. LAUNCH the application
2. BROWSE to a folder with video files
3. CLICK "Scan" to index your videos
4. SELECT a video to play it
5. USE "Find Duplicates" to find duplicates
6. CHECK boxes and DELETE unwanted files

================================================================================
KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
================================================================================

Space         - Play/Pause video
Escape        - Exit fullscreen
Double-click  - Toggle fullscreen
